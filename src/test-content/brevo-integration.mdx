## GTM Core Brevo Integration: Streamlining Transactional Emails with n8n

GTM Core is committed to providing robust tools that enhance your operational efficiency and customer engagement. We are pleased to announce the integration of Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) for managing your transactional emails, seamlessly powered by n8n. This update significantly enhances the reliability and automation of critical communications, ensuring your users receive timely and essential information without fail.

## What is the GTM Core Brevo Integration?

The GTM Core Brevo Integration is a new capability that allows our platform to leverage Brevo's powerful email service for delivering transactional emails. Transactional emails are non-promotional, one-to-one communications that are triggered by a user's action or a system event, such as password resets, order confirmations, account notifications, and shipping updates. By integrating Brevo, GTM Core ensures these vital messages are sent efficiently and reliably.

### Powering Transactional Emails with Brevo

Brevo is a leading email marketing and transactional email platform known for its high deliverability rates and scalable infrastructure. This integration harnesses Brevo's specialized capabilities to ensure that every critical message originating from GTM Core reaches its intended recipient promptly. You benefit from Brevo's robust sending infrastructure, detailed analytics, and adherence to email best practices, all designed to maintain strong sender reputation and optimal inbox placement.

### Seamless Automation with n8n

The backbone of this integration is n8n, a powerful workflow automation tool. n8n acts as the intelligent bridge, orchestrating the communication between GTM Core and Brevo. When an event occurs within GTM Core that requires a transactional email, n8n automatically triggers the appropriate workflow. This ensures data is securely passed to Brevo, which then dispatches the pre-configured email. This automation eliminates manual processes, reduces the chance of errors, and provides a flexible, scalable solution for your email delivery needs.

## How the Brevo Integration Works with GTM Core

The process is straightforward and designed for maximum efficiency:

1.  **Event Trigger:** An action or event occurs within GTM Core that necessitates sending a transactional email (e.g., new user registration, subscription update, system alert).
2.  **n8n Workflow Activation:** This event triggers a predefined workflow within n8n.
3.  **Data Transmission:** n8n securely extracts the necessary data from GTM Core and sends it to Brevo.
4.  **Email Dispatch:** Brevo receives the data and uses it to populate and send the appropriate transactional email template to the intended recipient.

This automated chain ensures that your transactional emails are sent reliably and consistently, without requiring constant manual oversight.
